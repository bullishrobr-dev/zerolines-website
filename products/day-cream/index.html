How it works

Protection mechanism

Environmental Shield Day Cream forms a breathable protective film on the skin surface. This film is designed to help shield against environmental particles and reduce moisture loss throughout the day.

The Pyrenean mineral water works at the skin level to support the skin's enzymatic defence systems. These minerals are essential cofactors for antioxidant enzymes that help neutralise free radicals generated by UV exposure and pollution.

Not a sunscreen

Environmental Shield Day Cream is designed to support the skin's defence against environmental stressors but is not a substitute for sunscreen. We recommend applying a broad-spectrum SPF after the cream has set. External use only.

Usage protocol

Application Protocol

Environmental Shield Day Cream follows a structured morning application protocol. It is the protective step that seals in the treatment layers while preparing the skin for the day ahead.

Morning application

Apply to face and neck every morning after The Serum. Use a pea-sized amount — sufficient to cover the entire face without excess.

Gentle upward massage

Massage gently in upward motions. This supports circulation and ensures even distribution without pulling the skin downward.

Allow to set

Wait 2–3 minutes before applying sunscreen or makeup. This allows the protective film to form and the minerals to begin their work.

Reapply if needed

In extremely dry or harsh environments, a light reapplication mid-day can help maintain the protective barrier and hydration levels.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it contain SPF?

No, Environmental Shield Day Cream does not contain SPF. It is designed as a protective layer that works alongside sunscreen. We recommend applying a broad-spectrum SPF after the cream has absorbed (2–3 minutes after application).

Is it heavy or greasy?

Environmental Shield Day Cream is formulated with a lightweight texture designed to absorb quickly without residue. It provides sustained hydration without the heaviness associated with traditional rich creams.

Can I wear makeup over it?

Yes. After allowing 2–3 minutes for absorption, Environmental Shield Day Cream provides a smooth, hydrated base for makeup application. The lightweight texture is designed not to interfere with foundation or other cosmetic products.

How long does one jar last?

With daily morning use of a pea-sized amount, one jar is designed to last approximately 2–3 months.

Do I need to reapply during the day?

In normal conditions, once-daily morning application is sufficient. In extremely dry, polluted, or harsh environments, a light reapplication mid-day can help maintain the protective barrier.

What makes the Pyrenean minerals special?

The Pyrenean mineral water is sourced from thermal springs at 1,800 meters above sea level. These minerals have a unique ionic profile that supports the skin's natural enzymatic defence systems.

Can I use it without The Serum?

While Environmental Shield Day Cream can be used independently, the Longevity Protocol is designed as an integrated system. The Serum provides the cellular treatment that the Environmental Shield Day Cream then protects. For best results, use both.

Is it suitable for oily skin?

Yes. The lightweight, fast-absorbing texture is designed for all skin types including oily and combination skin. It provides hydration without adding excess oil or shine.
