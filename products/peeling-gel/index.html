How it works

Gentle exfoliation mechanism

The Peeling Gel utilises a citric acid complex to gently dissolve the bonds between dead skin cells on the surface layer. This process supports the skin's natural shedding cycle without the need for physical abrasion.

After application, the formulation works within minutes to support surface renewal. Rinse thoroughly after the recommended contact time and follow with your treatment serum for optimal protocol synergy.

External use only

Peeling Gel is designed exclusively for external facial application. Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes. If irritation occurs, rinse immediately and discontinue use. Consult a healthcare professional if irritation persists.

Usage protocol

Application Protocol

Peeling Gel follows a structured weekly exfoliation protocol. Consistency and timing are designed to support progressive refinement without over-processing.

Weekly application

Apply to cleansed, dry face once per week. Gently massage in circular motions to support the exfoliation process.

Contact time

Leave on for 2 minutes maximum. Do not exceed the recommended contact time.

Rinse and continue

Rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water. Follow immediately with The Serum as the next step in the Longevity Protocol.

Consistent scheduling

Use on the same day each week. Many users prefer evening application to allow the skin to rest overnight.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use Peeling Gel?

Once per week only. The protocol is designed for weekly exfoliation to support natural renewal without over-processing. Using more frequently may compromise the skin barrier.

Can I use it with other exfoliating products?

We recommend avoiding other exfoliating products on the same day as Peeling Gel. The protocol is designed as a complete system — let this step do its work, then follow with The Serum.

Will it make my skin sensitive to the sun?

As with all exfoliation treatments, the skin may be more sensitive to UV exposure following application. We recommend using SPF during the day after your weekly exfoliation.

What should I apply after Peeling Gel?

Always follow Peeling Gel with The Serum (Step 2 of the Longevity Protocol). Freshly exfoliated skin is optimally positioned to receive the bioactive ingredients in the serum.

Is it suitable for sensitive skin?

The formulation is designed to be gentle, with a pH-balanced base and soothing botanical extracts. However, if you have particularly sensitive skin, we recommend performing a patch test before first use.

How long does one tube last?

With once-weekly use as directed, one tube is designed to last approximately 3–4 months.

Can I skip this step in the protocol?

While the protocol is designed as an integrated system, Peeling Gel prepares the skin for optimal absorption of subsequent treatments. For best results, we recommend following the complete 6-step protocol.

What results can I expect?

With consistent weekly use, Peeling Gel is designed to support smoother, more refined-looking skin over time. Individual results may vary depending on skin condition and consistency of use.
