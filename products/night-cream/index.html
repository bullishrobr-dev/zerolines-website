How it works

Overnight mechanism

During sleep, the skin's skin renewal processes are at their peak. Blood flow increases, cell turnover accelerates, and the skin enters a state of repair. Renewal and Repair Night Cream is formulated to support and enhance this natural window of renewal.

The hydrolyzed collagen and shea butter complex work together — hydrolyzed collagen supports skin comfort signalling while ceramides help rebuild the lipid barrier that may have been compromised during the day by environmental exposure. This dual approach supports both repair and protection.

External use only

Renewal and Repair Night Cream is designed exclusively for external facial and neck application. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional.

Usage protocol

Application Protocol

Renewal and Repair Night Cream follows a structured evening application protocol. It is the final step in the Longevity Protocol, designed to seal in all treatment layers and support overnight renewal.

Evening application

Apply as the final step in your evening routine, after The Serum. Use a slightly larger amount than the Environmental Shield Day Cream — approximately a almond-sized amount for face and neck.

Gentle massage

Massage gently into face and neck using upward motions. Take your time — this is a moment of care, not a race.

Allow to absorb

Allow 5–10 minutes for the cream to fully absorb before lying down. This prevents product transfer and ensures the active compounds begin their overnight work.

Sleep and renew

The cream works while you sleep. During the 6–8 hours of sleep, the formulation supports the skin's natural repair and renewal processes.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a separate Renewal and Repair Night Cream?

The skin's needs differ between day and night. During the day, the skin needs protection from environmental stressors. At night, the skin enters a repair mode with increased cell turnover. Renewal and Repair Night Cream is specifically formulated to support these overnight renewal processes.

Can I use my Environmental Shield Day Cream at night?

While you can, the Environmental Shield Day Cream is formulated for environmental protection — a function you don't need while sleeping. Renewal and Repair Night Cream contains hydrolyzed collagen and ceramides specifically designed to support the overnight repair cycle. For optimal protocol results, use the designated night formulation.

Will it stain my pillowcase?

Renewal and Repair Night Cream is designed to absorb fully within 5–10 minutes. We recommend applying it before your evening wind-down routine (reading, meditation, etc.) to allow full absorption before lying down.

How long does one jar last?

With nightly use of an almond-sized amount, one jar is designed to last approximately 2–3 months.

Is it too rich for oily skin?

Renewal and Repair Night Cream has a richer texture than Environmental Shield Day Cream by design — the skin can tolerate and benefits from more nourishment overnight. However, if you have very oily skin, start with a smaller amount and adjust as needed.

Can I use it with retinol?

The Longevity Protocol is designed as a complete system. If you currently use retinol, we recommend transitioning to the full protocol for a structured approach. If you wish to combine, use retinol and Renewal and Repair Night Cream on alternate nights.

What is the circadian renewal cycle?

The skin follows a circadian rhythm — a 24-hour cycle. During daylight hours, the skin is in defence mode. At night, blood flow increases, cell turnover accelerates, and repair processes activate. Renewal and Repair Night Cream is formulated to support this natural nighttime repair window.

Should I apply it to my neck too?

Yes. The neck skin is thinner and often shows signs of ageing earlier than the face. Apply Renewal and Repair Night Cream to both face and neck using upward massage motions for comprehensive overnight support.
