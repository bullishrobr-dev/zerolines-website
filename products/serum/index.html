How it works

Delivery mechanism

The Serum utilises a botanical peptide complex designed to mimic the skin's natural signalling molecules. These peptides communicate with fibroblast cells, supporting the processes involved in collagen synthesis and tissue repair.

Combined with hydrolyzed collagen, the formulation works at the skin level to support the skin's structural renewal. With consistent daily application, this supports progressive improvement in the visible appearance of firmness, texture, and overall skin quality.

External use only

The Serum is designed exclusively for external facial and neck application. Avoid contact with eyes. If irritation occurs, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional.

Usage protocol

Application Protocol

The Serum follows a structured daily application protocol. Consistency is essential for progressive cellular support.

Daily application

Apply 3–4 drops to cleansed face and neck every morning and/or evening. The protocol recommends twice-daily application for optimal results.

Press, don't rub

Gently press the serum into the skin using your fingertips. Pressing supports absorption without pulling or stretching the skin.

Allow absorption

Wait approximately 1 minute before applying your Environmental Shield Day Cream or Renewal and Repair Night Cream. This allows the active compounds to begin their work.

Long-term commitment

Cellular renewal is a gradual process. Consistent daily use for a minimum of 8–12 weeks supports the progressive refinement the protocol is designed to achieve.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long before I see results?

The Serum is designed for progressive cellular support. While some users report visible improvements in skin texture and radiance within a few weeks, the full benefits of skin renewal build over 8–12 weeks of consistent twice-daily use. Individual results may vary.

Can I use it with other serums?

The Serum is designed as a comprehensive treatment within the Longevity Protocol. We recommend using it as your primary active serum. If you wish to incorporate other products, apply them at a different time of day.

Should I use it morning or evening?

For optimal results, the protocol recommends twice-daily application — morning and evening. If you prefer once daily, evening application allows the active compounds to work during the skin's natural overnight renewal cycle.

How long does one bottle last?

With twice-daily use of 3–4 drops, one bottle is designed to last approximately 6–8 weeks.

Is it oily or heavy?

The Serum is formulated with a lightweight, fast-absorbing texture. It is designed to penetrate quickly without leaving a residue, making it suitable for layering under moisturiser and sunscreen.

Can I skip this step if I use Precision Collagen Activation Syringe?

Precision Collagen Activation Syringe and The Serum serve different purposes within the protocol. Precision Collagen Activation Syringe is a precision targeted treatment, while The Serum provides comprehensive facial and neck cellular support. For optimal protocol results, both are recommended.

Is it suitable for all skin types?

The Serum is designed for external facial application and is suitable for most skin types. The lightweight formulation is appropriate for oily, combination, and dry skin.

What happens if I miss a day?

Consistency supports optimal results. Missing an occasional day will not negate your progress, but regular daily application is recommended for the progressive cellular support the protocol is designed to provide.
