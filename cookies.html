Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Zero Lines uses them, and how you can manage your preferences.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you interact with content, and improve your overall experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site by collecting anonymous data.
  • Functionality cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language selection and cart contents.
  • Targeting cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness. We only use these with your consent.

Managing Your Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These providers have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our business practices. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@zerolines.com.
