Commitment

Accessibility

We believe beauty should be accessible to everyone.

Zero Lines is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Our Standards

We aim to conform to level AA of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone.

What We Do

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the site
  • Sufficient colour contrast ratios for readability
  • Text alternatives for all meaningful images
  • Focus indicators on all interactive elements
  • Respect for reduced-motion preferences
  • Clear, consistent navigation and labelling

Third-Party Content

Our website may contain links to third-party sites or embed third-party content. While we cannot control the accessibility of external platforms, we select partners who share our commitment to inclusivity.

Feedback & Support

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on our website, or if you have suggestions on how we can improve accessibility, please contact us. We take your feedback seriously and will make every reasonable effort to address your concerns.

Email: accessibility@zerolines.com

Assessment

We regularly review our website using automated tools and manual testing. Our accessibility statement was last reviewed in May 2025. We are committed to ongoing improvement and welcome your input.
